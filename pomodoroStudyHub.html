What is Pomodoro Study Hub?

Pomodoro Study Hub is an excellent method to enhance your concentration while studying. When you have your next study session, experiment with this structured approach involving shorter focused intervals, allowing your brain time for breaks.

How to use Pomodoro Study Hub?

Step 1
Select a single task to focus on
Step 2
Set a timer and work continously for 25 minutes
Step 3
Take a productive 5 minute break
Step 4
Repeat for 4 rounds
Step 5
Take a longer break for 30 minutes and repeat
